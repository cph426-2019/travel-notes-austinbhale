Study Abroad in Copenhagen

  1. Ride the "Vertigo", along with the other top scariest rides at Tivoli Gardens.
  2. Admire the strangeness and beauty of Freetown Christiana.
  3. Find all the giants, because how often do you get to say you're hunting giants?
  4. Finally see live electronic music at Copenhagen Distortion and a Slow Magic concert!
  5. Play sports with locals at a nearby park downtown.
  6. Dive into Nordic cuisine, especially the infamous Smørrebrøds.
  7. Learn how to create dynamic websites, since static is lame.
  8. Ride a bike around a city for the first time.
  9. Jump off a diving tower at one of the harbor pools.
  10. See some legit castles.
