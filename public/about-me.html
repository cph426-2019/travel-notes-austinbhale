About Me

Hello, and welcome to my travel blog site! My name's Austin, and I am a junior computer science major at UNC-Chapel Hill. For three weeks, I will be located in the beautiful city of Copenhagen as part of a study abroad program.

To give some insight about my background at UNC, I've worked as a Learning Assistant for COMP 401 as well as a Research Assistant for Dr. Gary Bishop and enabling technologies. I am involved in a few clubs at UNC, and the one that I cherish the most is the Enabling Technology Club. I serve as the Vice President and Treasurer where we promote the creation and use of enabling technologies. I am also involved in DevApp and Buckley Public Service Scholars.

This summer, I will be working as a JMP Technical Intern at SAS to update scripts for the documentation team's software. I'm excited to start this position as soon as I return home from Copenhagen.
