My Copenhagen Bucket List

  1. Ride the "Vertigo", along with the other top scariest rides at Tivoli Gardens.
  2. Admire the strangeness and beauty of Freetown Christiana. ✓
  3. Find all the giants, because how often do you get to say you're hunting giants?
  4. Finally see live electronic music at both Copenhagen Distortion and a Slow Magic concert! ✓
  5. Play sports with locals at a nearby park downtown or join a local gym. ✓
  6. Dive into Nordic cuisine, especially their infamous Smørrebrøds. ✓
  7. Learn how to create dynamic and responsive websites, since static is lame.
  8. Ride a bike or run around in the city. ✓
  9. Jump off a diving tower at one of the harbor pools.
  10. See some huge castles. ✓
